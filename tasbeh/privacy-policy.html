Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Islomov Odilxon Rustamovich ("Developer", "we", "us") built the Tasbeh application ("App") as a free application. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require user registration or an account.
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • Does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • Does not transmit any data over the internet.

2. Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences: Theme selection, animation choice, sound settings, vibration settings, font size, and button appearance.
  • Zikr (dhikr) data: Custom zikr entries you add, counting progress, and loop statistics.

This data is stored using Android's built-in SharedPreferences and Room database. It remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

3. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE: Used solely to provide haptic feedback when counting. This permission does not access, collect, or share any personal data.

The App does not request any dangerous permissions, network access, or permissions that could access personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party services that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The App is safe for users of all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach from our side. All data remains locally on your device and is protected by your device's own security measures (lock screen, encryption, etc.).

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App data through your device's Settings > Apps > Tasbeh > Storage > Clear Data.
  • Uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the App.
