2. Description of Service

QarzDaftar is a mobile application that provides the following functionality:

  • Debt & loan tracking — Record, manage, and monitor money you owe or are owed
  • Account management — Create debtor/creditor accounts with names, phone numbers, and notes
  • Transaction records — Log individual transactions with amounts, dates, descriptions, currency, and photo attachments
  • Google Drive backup & restore — Optionally back up your data to your own Google Drive account
  • Shared Ledger — Link with another user to synchronize a shared debt account via Google Drive
  • Export — Export records to Excel (XLSX) or PDF format
  • QR code sharing — Share account information via QR codes
  • Biometric lock — Secure the App with fingerprint or face recognition
  • Multi-language support — Available in 21 languages including English, Russian, Uzbek, Turkish, Arabic, Hindi, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Chinese, Korean, Kazakh, Kyrgyz, Tajik, Turkmen, Azerbaijani, Persian, Japanese, German, Indonesian, and Bengali
