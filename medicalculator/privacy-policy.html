Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

MediCalculator ("the App") is a medical calculator application for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The App is designed to operate entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers.

  • Calculation history & favorites — stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device.
  • Preferences (theme, language) — stored locally on your device.
  • No accounts or sign-ups — the App does not require or create user accounts.

2. Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad relevance

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before personalized advertising. You may choose non-personalized ads or withdraw consent at any time through the App's settings.

Premium users who purchase the "Remove Ads" option will not see any ads, and AdMob will not be initialized.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads, processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Google. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (history, favorites, preferences) can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or California, you have the right to:

  • Access the data held about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights by clearing app data or adjusting ad consent in the App's settings.

8. Security

The App stores data only on your device using Android's standard encrypted storage mechanisms. No data is transmitted to external servers controlled by us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
